Biography

I am a professional uilleann piper with several years of experience. Born and raised in Cork, Ireland, I started playing traditional Irish music at a young age.

Throughout my career, I have collaborated with various musicians and performed at numerous festivals and events in Ireland and abroad.

My passion for music led me to explore different genres and incorporate modern elements into traditional Irish music, creating a unique and captivating sound.
